ABOUT BANGKETA ESKWELA


How We Started

Bangketa Eskwela started in San Fernando, Pampanga. It is a Non-Government, Non-Profit Organization established by Dara Mae H. Tuazon in 2017, and registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission in 2018. Its mission is to develop concrete, efficient and effective programs for street children, out-of-school youth and indigenous children and see them with transformed lives, having a passion for education, and committed to being agents of social change and nation-building through volunteerism.

These programs consist of Educating the Youth, Financial Assistance, Good Parenting Seminars, Supplemental Feeding, Christmas Gift Giving and Values Formation, Volunteers Team Awareness, and Building Program and Spiritual Enhancement fellowship.

VISION

We see children with transformed lives, having passion for education, and committed to be agents of social change and nation building through volunteerism.

MISSION

To instill love for education among children, help transform their lives for a brighter future and committed to be agents of change and nation building.
